About

I am a lecturer (assistant professor) in Economics and Data Science at the Department of Economics at Lancaster University Management School. I hold a Ph.D. in Economics from Bocconi University. I am an applied microeconomist with research interests in political economics.

In my research, I take advantage of text analysis, computer vision and computer audition techniques to study how different types of media coverage affect voters' and politicians' behaviors.
