Work in Progress

Do TV News Chase Twitter? A High-Frequency Analysis

I propose an original measure of TV coverage of @realDonaldTrump tweets using narrative texts displayed on the lower-third of TV screens. This allows me to have a high-frequency measure of news coverage. I study if and how cable TV channels respond to tweets by the President in real time, using narrow time windows. I find that, in the aggregate, FNC devotes more time to Trump coverage minutes after a generic tweet by the President while CNN and MSNBC seem to be insensitive. I then distinguish between different anchors within networks and find that CNN and MSNBC also react. CNN's Jake Tapper and MSNBC's Chuck Todd decrease Trump coverage minutes after a @realDonaldTrump tweet.
